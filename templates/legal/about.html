How it started

Motor Match started after one too many frustrating experiences on bloated, ad-heavy car marketplaces where you could never tell if a listing was genuine or how old it was. We built something cleaner from scratch — a platform focused entirely on the private car sale experience.

We're a small, UK-based team that cares about performance, privacy, and making the process of buying and selling a used car actually pleasant.
