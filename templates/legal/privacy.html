Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Motor Match ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal data you provide when using our platform. We are committed to handling your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who is the data controller?

Motor Match is the data controller for personal data processed through this platform. If you have any questions about how we use your data, contact us at privacy@motormatch.co.uk.

2. What data we collect

We collect and store the following personal data when you use Motor Match:

Account & profile

  • Email address — used for login, notifications, and account recovery
  • First and last name — displayed on your public profile and listings
  • Password — stored as a one-way bcrypt hash; we never store your plain-text password
  • Profile photo — uploaded and served via Cloudinary (cloud image hosting)
  • Phone number — optionally provided; displayed on listings if you choose to show it
  • Location — town or city you optionally enter on your profile
  • Bio — a short free-text description you optionally write on your profile

Vehicle listings

  • Listing data: make, model, year, mileage, fuel type, transmission, body type, colour, price, condition, description, and location
  • Listing photos: uploaded and stored via Cloudinary
  • DVLA lookup: if you use the registration plate lookup, the plate is sent to the DVLA API to pre-fill listing fields. We do not store the raw registration number independently of the listing you create.

Conversations & messaging

  • Messages: the full text of messages sent between buyers and sellers, stored in our database
  • GIF attachments: GIF URLs chosen via the Tenor integration, stored with the message record
  • Message reactions: emoji characters and which user placed them, stored per-message
  • Read state: whether you have read each message (used to power unread counts)

Activity & preferences

  • Saved vehicles: listings you save to your watchlist — stored in our database linked to your account
  • Bids / offers: offer amounts you place on listings, with timestamps, retained even after a listing closes
  • Recently viewed: the last several listings you viewed, stored temporarily in your browser session
  • Notifications: records of platform notifications sent to you (message received, offer placed, listing updated)

Security & technical

  • Login events & IP addresses: we record failed and successful login attempts via django-axes to protect against brute-force attacks
  • Session cookie: a server-side session identifier stored in a browser cookie to keep you logged in
  • CSRF token: a security token stored in a cookie to prevent cross-site request forgery attacks

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your vehicle listings to other users
  • Enable messaging between buyers and sellers
  • Send you notifications about activity on your listings or messages
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for automated profiling or sell it to third parties.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: processing necessary to provide the Service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests: security monitoring, fraud prevention, and platform improvement
  • Consent: where you have explicitly opted in to communications
  • Legal obligation: where we are required by law to retain or share data

5. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Deleted listings are retained in our database for up to 30 days before permanent deletion, to support dispute resolution. Message history is retained for 12 months. Usage logs are retained for up to 90 days.

6. Who we share data with

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We use the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Cloudinary — stores and serves your profile photo and listing images. Images are uploaded to Cloudinary's CDN. See Cloudinary's Privacy Policy.
  • Tenor (Google) — if you use the GIF search in messaging, your search query is sent to Tenor's API. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • DVLA API — registration plate lookups during listing creation are forwarded to the DVLA's public vehicle enquiry API. No personal data is sent, only the plate number.
  • Infrastructure providers: our hosting and database providers process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • Law enforcement: where we are legally required to disclose information.

7. Cookies and tracking

We use the following cookies. We do not use advertising networks, third-party tracking pixels, or cookies that follow you across other websites.

  • Session cookie (sessionid): an HTTP-only cookie that identifies your login session. It expires when you sign out or your session times out. This cookie is strictly necessary.
  • CSRF cookie (csrftoken): a security cookie that prevents cross-site request forgery. Required for all form submissions. This cookie is strictly necessary.
  • Cookie consent (mm_cookie_consent): stored in your browser's localStorage (not a cookie) to remember whether you have acknowledged this banner. Expires when you clear site data.

Because we use only strictly necessary cookies and no analytics or advertising cookies, we only require your acknowledgement rather than explicit opt-in consent under the UK PECR. You may clear cookies at any time in your browser settings.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your personal data — you can do this directly via Settings → Delete Account. This immediately anonymises your email, name, phone, bio, location, and avatar, and removes all your active listings. Message history is anonymised in-place.
  • Restriction: ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@motormatch.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Data security

We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords, CSRF protection, and rate limiting on sensitive endpoints. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at privacy@motormatch.co.uk.
